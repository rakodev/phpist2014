Transportation

From Kadikoy

Kadikoy - Besiktas ferry departing from the ferry port to the beach will bring you Besiktas. When you arrive to Besiktas, venue will be in the direction of ~ 2 minutes walk from Ortakoy.

From Mecidiyeköy

You can arrive to the venue by using the bus with number 30M.

From Esenler/Otogar

1. First you need to use Havalimanı-Aksaray metro line. You can transfer to T1 Kabatas-Bagcilar tram at Yusufpasa station by five minutes of walking or Aksaray station by ten minutes of walking. After you use and arrive to Kabatas any bus you use will get you to Besiktas. Once you arrive Besiktas you can find the event place by two minutes of walking to Ortakoy direction.

2. You can transfer to Avcılar-Zincirlikuyu metrobus line at Zeytinburnu station after once you use Havalimani-Aksaray metro. When you get off the metrobus at Zincirlikuyu every bus goes to Besiktas and Kabatas will get you to Besiktas. Once you arrived to Besiktas you can arrive to event by two minutes of walking.

From Ataturk Airport

Participants coming from Ataturk Airport can use the same way with the participants coming from Esenler/Otogar.

From Pendik

You can transfer to Sogutlucesme-Zincirlikuyu metrobus line by using Pendik-Haydarpasa train. After you arrive Zincirlikuyu every bus goes to Besiktas and Kabatas will get you to Besiktas. Once you arrived to Besiktas you can arrive to event by two minutes of walking.

From Kartal

1. You can transfer to Sogutlucesme-Zincirlikuyu metrobus line by using Pendik-Haydarpasa train. After you arrive Zincirlikuyu every bus goes to Besiktas and Kabatas will get you to Besiktas. Once you arrived to Besiktas you can arrive to event by two minutes of walking.

2. You can get Kadikoy - Besiktas ferry taking Kadikoy - Kartal metro line. After crossing to Besiktas coast(shore) taking uskudar besiktas ferry, you can arrive approximately in two minutes walking toward Ortakoy.

From Yenibosna

After debussing metrobus in Zincirlikuyu you can get Besiktas square with any bus headed Besiktas and Kabatas. Than after nearly 2 minutes of walking toward Ortakoy you can arrive in.

From Fatih / Edirnekapı

You can take bus with line number 28 to get Besiktas coast(shore).Than after nearly 2 minutes of walking toward Ortakoy u can arrive in.

From Üsküdar

After crossing to Besiktas coast(shore) taking Uskudar-Besiktas ferry, you can arrive approximately in two minutes walking toward Ortakoy.
